Why this summit matters

Cyber Summit 2026 brings students, mentors, educators, and professionals together for immersive workshops in cybersecurity, AI, ethical hacking, and hardware security.

This event is built for both curious beginners and experienced student technologists who want real technical challenges, guided learning, and credible pathways into cyber careers.

Workshop Highlights

Cyber Escape Room

Team-based cyber puzzles featuring password cracking, log analysis, cryptography, and forensics.

AI Capture The Flag

Analyze and defend AI systems while exploring prompt injection, model manipulation, and adversarial concepts.

AI Hardware Lab

Connect emotion-recognition AI outputs to Arduino-driven hardware for real-time physical reactions.

IoT Penetration Testing Lab

Explore smart-device vulnerabilities and beginner pentesting concepts through guided, hands-on labs.
